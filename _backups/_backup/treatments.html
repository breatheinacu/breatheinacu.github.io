Treatments

Acupuncture:

Each session begins with a comprehensive health history intake followed by a customized treatment to meet your individual needs.  Lay.  Relax.  Enjoy.

  • Initial treatment: 75 min
  • Follow-up treatments: 60 min
Constitutional Acupuncture Facial Renewal:

Each treatment begins with a health history intake.  Customized herbal masks are applied to the face, hair-like needles are inserted to points throughout the body and face specifically tailored to each individual. Tui-na acupressure wrinkle treatment is applied to face, neck, shoulders and head.  Lay. Relax. Rejuvenate.

Ten treatments recommended.
  • 60 min
  • 10 sessions
Ultra Relaxing Warm Cupping Treatment:

Each treatment entails the sliding of several warm glass cups suctioned to the skin along the back, neck, shoulders, arms and legs. Relaxing, detoxifying and therapeutic for anyone who is active or stressed. Perfect for back pain, sciatic pain or tight muscles.

  • 30 min
Auricular Acupuncture:

Each treatment begins with a brief health history intake. Needles are inserted to specific points on the ears, along with a few selected body points. Lay. Relax. Enjoy.

Recommended for stress relief, smoking cessation, caffeine addiction and general detoxification.

  • Initial treatment: 45 min
  • Follow-up treatments: 30 min

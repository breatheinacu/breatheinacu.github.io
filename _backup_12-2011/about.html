WHO AM I

Lexi Hagenson is a licensed acupuncturist and board certified herbalist. She graduated from Pacific College of Oriental Medicine in New York City with a masters in Traditional Oriental Medicine. The rigorous four year program at Pacific included various acupuncture styles and techniques, Oriental medicine theory, Chinese herbology, anatomy, physiology, pathophysiology and nutrition. Lexi is certified by the National Certification Commission for Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (NCCAOM). She also holds a bachelors degree in psychology from the University of Wisconsin- Madison.
